Our Story

Healing Lives Since 1985

Sita Superspeciality Hospital was founded with a single mission: to provide accessible, high-quality healthcare to every member of our community — regardless of background or circumstance.

Today, we stand as the region's premier medical institution, with cutting-edge facilities, a distinguished medical faculty, and an unwavering commitment to clinical excellence.

  • Accredited by the Joint Commission on Medical Excellence
  • Top-rated in patient satisfaction for 10 consecutive years
  • Home to the region's leading research & teaching programs
  • Committed to sustainable, eco-conscious healthcare operations

Milestones

Our Journey

1985

Founded

Sita Superspeciality Hospital opened its doors with 50 beds and a team of 30 dedicated medical professionals.

Launched the region's first dedicated cardiac surgery center, performing over 500 open-heart procedures in its inaugural year.

Common Emergencies

When to Go to the ER

💔

Chest Pain

Sudden chest pain, pressure, or discomfort — especially with shortness of breath — requires immediate care.

🧠

Stroke Symptoms

Sudden confusion, facial drooping, arm weakness, or speech difficulty. Remember: Act FAST.

🩸

Severe Bleeding

Uncontrolled bleeding from injury or surgery. Apply pressure and come to our ER immediately.

🌡️

High Fever

Fever above 103°F (39.4°C) in adults, or any fever in infants under 3 months, warrants ER evaluation.
